Focus Timer

Focus Timer is a simple pomodoro-based tool designed to help you stay productive. Use focused 25-minute work sessions with short breaks to improve concentration and reduce burnout.

Why Use Focus Timer?

The Pomodoro Technique is a proven method for enhancing focus, productivity, and mental clarity. By breaking work into focused 25-minute sessions followed by short breaks, you train your brain to avoid distractions and stay engaged.

Focus Timer makes this technique simple and accessible — with a clean interface, no distractions, and no unnecessary features. It's perfect for studying, deep work, writing, or creative tasks where staying present matters most.
