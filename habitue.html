Frequently Asked

Can I track habits multiple times a day?

Yes. Create habits with counts or time goals and log completions whenever they happen.

Does Habitue sync across devices?

Habitue stores data securely on your device. iCloud support is on the roadmap, and exports let you keep backups today.

Can I pause or archive a habit?

Absolutely. Hide habits you’re not tracking right now without losing history or streaks.
