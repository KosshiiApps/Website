FAQs

Quick answers

Installing & updating

All apps ship through the App Store. If an update stalls, restart the App Store and try again on Wi‑Fi.

Crashes or sync issues

Force-quit and relaunch first. If it repeats, email me with the steps you took and your device model/iOS version.

Notifications

Check iOS Settings > Notifications for the app. Ensure Focus modes or Low Power aren’t blocking alerts.

Data, export, backups

Most data stays on-device. If you need an export, reach out with the app name and what you want to export.

Subscription or billing

Billing is handled by Apple. You can manage or cancel from Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

Platform availability

Apps are designed for iPhone first. iPad and Mac support varies by app; check the app page for specifics.

Known limitations

If a feature is limited by design, I’ll note it on the app page. Still unsure? Send a quick email.

Feature requests

I prioritize clarity and reliability. If you have an idea, include the problem it solves so I can weigh it thoughtfully.
