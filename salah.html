Salah

A beautifully minimal prayer time app with accurate local timings, Qibla compass, and a clean interface — designed to help you stay consistent and spiritually connected.

Why Use Salah?

Prayer is a central part of daily life — but with a busy schedule, it can be easy to miss a time or forget direction while traveling.

The Salah app helps by providing accurate reminders, a reliable Qibla compass, and a distraction-free interface focused on what matters.
