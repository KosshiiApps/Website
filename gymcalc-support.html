FAQs

Quick answers

Installing & updating

Download via the App Store. If an update stalls, retry on Wi‑Fi and restart the App Store app.

Crashes or freezes

Force-quit and relaunch. If it repeats, email device model, iOS version, and the steps that trigger it.

Calculation accuracy

Check your bar type and plate set in settings. If numbers seem off, send your inputs and expected output.

Units and conversions

Switch between kg and lb in app settings. Conversions use standard 1 kg = 2.20462 lb.

Saved presets

If a preset won’t load, try re-saving it. Email me if it still fails and include the load and bar you’re using.

Notifications

If you use reminders, ensure iOS Notifications are enabled and Focus modes aren’t blocking alerts.

Data & privacy

GymCalc keeps data minimal. See the privacy policy for details.

Feature requests

I prioritize clarity and speed. Tell me the problem you want solved and I’ll consider it for the roadmap.
