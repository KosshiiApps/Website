Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

A time management method that uses 25-minute focused sessions followed by 5-minute breaks to improve concentration and reduce burnout.

Can I adjust timer durations?

Currently, the app is designed to keep things minimal with fixed intervals. A future update may include customization options.

Does the app run in the background?

Yes! If you start a session and switch apps, the timer continues and you'll get notified when time is up.

Why didn't I get a notification?

  • Ensure Focus Timer notifications are enabled in iOS Settings ‚Üí Notifications
  • Make sure Do Not Disturb or Focus Mode isn't blocking alerts
  • Try restarting the app

Does this app store any of my data?

No. Your session history and preferences are stored on-device and never leave your phone.
