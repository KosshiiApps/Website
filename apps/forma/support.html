Forma Support

Need help logging sets or staying on track? These quick answers will help you get the most out of Forma.

Common Questions

How do I log a set during my workout?

Tap the exercise you’re working on and enter reps and weight. Forma remembers your last entry so the next set is just a couple of taps away.

Can I edit or delete previous workouts?

Yes. Open History from the home screen, select the workout, and you can adjust sets, notes, or remove an exercise entirely.

How do I add custom exercises?

From the workout builder, tap New Exercise, name it, choose a category or muscle group, and it will be ready for future sessions.
