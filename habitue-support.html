Common Questions

  • How do I track a habit? – Tap on the habit and log your completion each day with a single tap.
  • Can I edit past dates? – Yes! Use the calendar view to log missed or completed days.
  • How do I enable reminders? – Head to Settings → Notifications and toggle reminders for any habit.
