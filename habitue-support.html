Common Questions

How do I track a habit?

Tap on the habit and log your completion each day with a single tap.

Can I edit past dates?

Yes! Use the calendar view to log missed or completed days.

How do I enable reminders?

Head to Settings ‚Üí Notifications and toggle reminders for any habit.

How do I add a new habit?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen and create your custom habit with optional reminders and goals.

Can I see my progress over time?

Absolutely! Check the insights tab to view weekly, monthly, and yearly progress charts for all your habits.

How do streaks work?

A streak is the number of consecutive days you've completed a habit. Missing a day resets the streak, but don't worry ‚Äì consistency over perfection!

Can I export my habit data?

Yes! Use the export feature in Settings to save your habit data as a CSV file for backup or analysis.
