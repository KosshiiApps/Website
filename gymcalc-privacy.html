Privacy Policy

GymCalc Privacy

GymCalc is built to be quick, clear, and respectful of your data. Here’s exactly what is collected, why, and how you stay in control.

Last updated

December 2025

Introduction

GymCalc is made by a one-person indie builder focused on simple, focused tools. Privacy is kept minimal and intentional: only what’s needed to support the app and improve it over time.

Information I Collect

  • Personal data you provide: If you email me for support (name, email, any details you share). Used only to respond and resolve issues.
  • Usage data: Privacy-conscious analytics about app performance and feature usage (e.g., screens visited, crashes). Collected in aggregate to improve stability and design.
  • Device info: Non-identifying details like device model, OS version, and language to keep the app reliable across setups.

How Information Is Used

  • Improve GymCalc: Understand what features help most, fix crashes, and keep the experience fast.
  • Support and communication: Reply to your questions, share important fixes, and follow up on bug reports.
  • Reliability and safety: Monitor performance, prevent abuse, and keep the app stable.

Sharing & Disclosure

  • No selling of data. Your information is never sold.
  • Limited third parties: Essential service providers (e.g., analytics, crash reporting) may process data solely to operate and improve GymCalc.
  • Legal requirements: Information may be shared if required by law or to protect users and the service.

Data Security

Data is sent over encrypted connections where possible, stored with minimal access, and limited to what’s necessary. No system is perfectly secure; if an issue arises, I will communicate steps to address it.

Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of personal data you’ve shared. Email kosshiiapps@gmail.com and I’ll respond promptly.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates will appear here with a new “Last updated” date. For meaningful changes, I’ll post a short notice so you can review them.

Contact

Privacy questions? Email kosshiiapps@gmail.com. Responses typically arrive within 1–2 business days.
