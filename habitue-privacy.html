Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2025

We value your privacy. Habitue does not collect or store any personal data.

What We Store

  • Your habit names and completion history (stored locally on your device)
  • App preferences and settings (stored locally)
  • Reminder preferences (stored locally)

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to servers or the cloud.

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Any data that could identify you

Data Backup and Export

You can export your habit data as a CSV file using the app's export feature. This file is created locally and shared through iOS's standard share sheet - you control where it goes.

Third-Party Services

Habitue does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media SDKs
  • External tracking tools

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from users of any age. It is suitable for all ages and contains no objectionable content.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top. Continued use of the app means you accept the revised terms.
