Privacy Policy

Built for trust

Every app and page is designed to be simple, focused, and respectful of your data. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you stay in control.

Last updated

December 2025

Introduction

I'm a one-person indie maker. I treat privacy like I would want mine treated: minimal collection, clear purpose, and no surprises. When you use the KosshiiApps website or apps, you can expect straightforward communication about how your information is handled.

Information I Collect

  • Personal data you provide: If you email me (e.g., your name, email address, and any details you share). Used only to reply and support you.
  • Usage data: Basic analytics to understand site traffic and app health (e.g., page views, device type, general performance events). Data is collected in aggregate and not sold.
  • Device information: Non-identifying details like browser, operating system, and language to keep experiences reliable.

How Information Is Used

  • Improve the apps and site: Spot issues, understand what helps people most, and refine the experience.
  • Support and communication: Respond to questions, share important updates, and follow up on bug reports.
  • Reliability and safety: Monitor performance, prevent abuse, and keep the service stable.

Sharing & Disclosure

  • No selling of data. Your information is never sold.
  • Limited third parties: Privacy-conscious analytics or infrastructure providers may process data to deliver the service. They receive only what is necessary to operate the app or site.
  • Legal requirements: Information may be shared if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users.

Data Security

Data is handled with minimal collection, access controls, and encrypted transport (HTTPS) wherever possible. Even with safeguards, no system is perfectly secure; if an issue arises, I will communicate promptly with next steps.

Your Rights

You can request to access, correct, or delete personal information you have shared. Email kosshiiapps@gmail.com with your request and I’ll handle it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date. If the changes are significant, I’ll share a short notice on the site so you can review them.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email kosshiiapps@gmail.com. I aim to reply within 1–2 business days.
